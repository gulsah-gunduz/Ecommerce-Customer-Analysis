# Ecommerce-Customer-Analysis

 Project Overview
 This project analyzes over 95,000+ customers and 112,000+ orders to identify high-value segments and optimize marketing strategies with useing  SQL and visualizes insights using Power BI.

 Key Analyses
- Customer segmentation based on order frequency and spending
- Revenue distribution across customer segments
- Identification of high-value customers

Key Insights
- Segment Efficiency: Identified that the "New but High Potential" segment generates 60% of total revenue despite making up only 10% of the customer base.
- High-potential customers contribute more revenue than expected
- Customer segmentation helps identify growth opportunities

 Business Recommendations
- Target high-potential customers with retention campaigns
- Convert high-value customers into loyal customers
- Improve engagement for low-value segments
